Frequently Asked Questions

Is this really free?

Yes! Completely free with 25% CPU, 128 MB RAM, and 250 MB storage. Perfect for small to medium Discord bots serving hundreds of users.

Do I need to renew the bot hosting?

Yes, free Discord bot hosting needs to be renewed every 24 hours to keep it online 24/7. This helps us manage resources fairly for all users.

Can I upload my existing bot?

Upload whatever you want - bot files, dependencies, databases. As long as it follows our Terms of Service, you're good to go.

Can I earn money with my free bot?

No way. Free bot hosting is for personal use only - we're strict about this and will shut down commercial bots.

What happens if I don't renew?

Your bot will go offline after 24 hours. Your files are safe if you renew within 28 days of expiry! Just renew to bring it back online - takes less than 10 seconds.

Where are your servers hosted?

We've got servers in the US (Salt Lake City) and Netherlands (The Hague) to ensure low latency. We unfortunately have no servers in Asia due to the high costs involved.
